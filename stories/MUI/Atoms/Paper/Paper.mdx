# Paper

A themed surface container that lifts content off the background using a shadow or border. Use Paper to group related content into a visually distinct region.

---

## Usage

```tsx
import { Paper } from '@bmi/tonic-components-mui';

<Paper elevation={2} sx={{ p: 3 }}>
  Content here
</Paper>
```

## Variants

Paper exposes two visual variants. Use `elevation` (default) to raise the surface with a drop shadow, or `outlined` for a flat bordered surface when vertical hierarchy is not needed.

## Elevations

Elevation 0–4 maps directly to the four Tonic shadow tokens. Increase elevation to signal greater depth or importance. On light backgrounds, prefer elevations 1–2 for cards and panels; reserve 3–4 for modals and floating surfaces.
